Begining

The Pérola group began its business project in 1996 with the creation of Bodegas Fernández de Piérola in the DOCa Rioja, the easternmost part of the Alavesa sub-zone.

Values

The Group's wines stand out for the balance between the traditional values ​​of land and variety,as well as the most advanced technologies of modern oenology.

Thinking to nature

Grupo Piérola is also committed to sustainable development, so the new TRASLASCUESTAS winery has been conceived as an eco-efficient building that respects the environment, which manages to reach 100% of its thermal requirements thanks to geothermal energy, positioning itself as a winery pioneer in its application in the Denomination of Origin Ribera de Duero.
